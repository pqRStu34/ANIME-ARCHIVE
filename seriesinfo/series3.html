Hinako Note

Slice of Life Comedy 2017

Description

Hinako Sakuragi, a shy and soft-spoken girl who has trouble talking to people, moves from the countryside to Tokyo to attend high school and join the theater club. Hoping to overcome her social anxiety, she stays at Hitotose Manor, a boarding house where she meets several unique residents. Among them are the energetic and supportive Kuina Natsukawa, the mature and reliable Mayuki Hiiragi, and the calm landlady Chiaki Hagino. Together, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and fun-filled stage performances.
